About Us

 

BusinessReach is a Silicon Valley based Business Networking Services Company. We founded BusinessReach to enable people in business and companies to connect with each other to drive the speed of business.

 

We are a team of Silicon Valley technologists and business leaders. We formed our experiences in the world of global companies that serve customers world-wide. We also recognized that the way business is done today, with paper, email, and isolated silos of information, hinders the speed with which we could conduct our roles and serve our customers. What drives us is the need to connect companies to form a seamless web of business relationships that enables information to be exchanged freely, without limitations of communications.
