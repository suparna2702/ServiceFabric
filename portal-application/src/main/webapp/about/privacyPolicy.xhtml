About Us

 

Last modified: July 2012

There are many different ways you can use our services – to search for and share information, to communicate with other people or to create and exchange new content. When you share information with us, for example by creating a user or business profile, we can make those services even better – to show you more relevant search results and ads, to help you connect with people or to make sharing with others quicker and easier.

Our Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect and why we collect it.
  • How we use that information.
  • The choices we offer, including how to access and update information.

Information we collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users – from determine where you are located, to more complex things like which ads you’ll find most useful or the people who matter most to you online.

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you give us. For example, our personal and business profiles ask for information like your name, email address, telephone number or credit card. If you want to take full advantage of the sharing features we offer, we might also ask you to create a publicly visible profile which may include your name and photo.
  • Information we obtain from your use of our services. We may collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, like when you visit the profile of another member or business entity, or the services of affiliates on BusinessReach. This information includes:
    • Device information

We may collect device-specific information (such as your device manufacturer and model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information including phone number). BusinessReach may associate your device identifiers or phone number with your profile.

    • Log information

When you use our services or view content provided by BusinessReach, we may automatically collect and store certain information on our systems. These may include:

      • details of how you used our service, such as your search queries.
      • The addresses on the internet (IP Address) from where you have connected to our services
      • cookies that may uniquely identify your browser or your BusinessReach Account.
    • Location information

When you provide detailed information about your location, we may correlate this with other information such as IP Addresses to further confirm your location.

    • Local storage

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage (including HTML 5) and application data caches.

    • Cookies and anonymous identifiers

We use various technologies to collect and store information when you visit BusinessReach, and this may include sending one or more cookies or other anonymous identifiers on your devices.

How we use information we collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect BusinessReach and our users. We also use this information to offer you targeted content

We may use the name you provide for your BusinessReach Profile across all of the services we offer. If other users already have your email, or other information that identifies you, we may show them your publicly visible BusinessReach Profile information, such as your name and photo.

When you contact BusinessReach, we may keep a record of your communication to help solve any issues you might be facing. We may use your email address to inform you about our services, such as letting you know about upcoming changes or improvements.

We use information collected from cookies to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. For example, by saving your language preferences, we’ll be able to have our services appear in the language you prefer. When showing you tailored ads, we will not associate a cookie or anonymous identifier with sensitive categories, such as those based on race, religion, sexual orientation or health.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

BusinessReach may process personal information on our servers in many countries around the world. We may process your personal information on a server located outside the country where you live.

Information you share

Most of our services allow you to share information with others. Remember that when you share information publicly, it may be indexable by search engines. Our services provide you with different options on sharing and removing your content.

Accessing and updating your personal information

Whenever you use our services, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information. If that information is wrong, we strive to give you ways to update it quickly or to delete it – unless we have to keep that information for legitimate business or legal purposes. When updating your personal information, we may ask you to verify your identity before we can act on your request.

We may reject requests that are unreasonably repetitive, require disproportionate technical effort (for example, developing a new system or fundamentally changing an existing practice), risk the privacy of others, or would be extremely impractical

Where we can provide information access and correction, we will do so for free, except where it would require a disproportionate effort. We aim to maintain our services in a manner that protects information from accidental or malicious destruction. Because of this, after you delete information from our services, we may not immediately delete residual copies from our active servers and may not remove information from our backup systems.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals outside of BusinessReach unless one of the following circumstances apply:

  • With your consent

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of BusinessReach when we have your consent to do so. We require opt-in consent for the sharing of critical personal information

  • With Business Organization Owners

If your personal profile is associated with a business, and this business profile is managed for you by an administrator then your administrator will have access to your Personal Profile information. Your administrator may be able to:

    • view statistics regarding your account, like statistics regarding applications you use
    • suspend or terminate your membership to the business organization.

 

·         For external processing

We provide personal information to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons to process it for us, based on our instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

  • For legal reasons

We will share personal information with companies, organizations or individuals outside of BusinessReach if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

    • meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
    • enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations.
    • detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.
    • protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of BusinessReach, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

We may share aggregated, non personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners – like publishers, advertisers or connected sites. For example, we may share information publicly to show trends about the general use of our services.

If BusinessReach is involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Information security

We work hard to protect BusinessReach and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt access to certain portions of our services using SSL
  • We review our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorized access to systems.
  • We restrict access to personal information to BusinessReach employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.

Application

Our Privacy Policy applies to all of the services offered by BusinesReach Inc. and its affiliates, including services offered on other sites (such as our advertising services), but excludes services that have separate privacy policies that do not incorporate this Privacy Policy.

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to services offered by other companies or individuals, including products or sites that may be displayed to you in search results, sites that may include BusinessReach services, or other sites linked from our services. Our Privacy Policy does not cover the information practices of other companies and organizations who advertise our services, and who may use cookies, pixel tags and other technologies to serve and offer relevant ads.

Enforcement

We regularly review our compliance with our Privacy Policy. When we receive formal written complaints, we will contact the person who made the complaint to follow up. We work with the appropriate regulatory authorities, including local data protection authorities, to resolve any complaints regarding the transfer of personal data that we cannot resolve with our users directly.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes). We will also keep prior versions of this Privacy Policy in an archive for your review.
