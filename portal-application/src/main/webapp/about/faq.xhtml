  2. Why Join BusinessReach?

    1. Why should I become a member?

      Our service is free for individual members, forever! We will eventually offer additional services that you can pay to enjoy additional benefits from our service platform. However, basic membership will always be free of charge

      In BusinessReach, you can find other businesses and individuals interested in business that will allow you to improve how you deliver goods and services in your company, again at no charge to you

    2. I am a Reseller, why should my company join BusinessReach?

      Our service platform allows Resellers to find new products or suppliers to offer to their customers.

      • Find new Suppliers for new products to sell in your regions

      • Make your business easy to find by listing your locations within BusinessReach as well as the partner finder pages of your Distributors and Suppliers, with a single entry. If you add a new location, enter once and allow BusinessReach to update information across all Distributors and Suppliers

      • if your existing suppliers are in our system, connecting with them in BusinessReach enables these Suppliers to provide information about their products and services to you in a way that greatly simplifies how you manage your business.

      3.  

    3. I am a Distributor, why should my company join BusinessReach?

      Our service platform allows Distributors to find new products or Suppliers to offer to their customers.

      • If you are seeking to add new Resellers, you can search our community of Resellers to invite them to join your Distributorship as a formal reseller for the products and services you offer.

      • Build powerful and easy to update Partner finder pages. Since BusinessReach is an online community, each time one of your Resellers adds a new location, this information will be automatically updated on your partner pages, eliminating errors and increasing customer traction.

      • Offer Resellers the power of the BusinessReach system to send leads, offer product information, run campaigns and simplify how they manage their business.

      • If your existing suppliers are in our system, connecting with them in BusinessReach enables these Suppliers to provide information about their products and services to you in a way that greatly simplifies how you manage your business.

      • Find new distributors or Resellers from our large community

      • Search our community to identify highly performing Resellers and Distributors based on their profiles, reviews and their existing reach to other businesses

      • Use our partner management system to invite, recruit and manage your partner relationships with Resellers and Distributors

      • Build powerful and easy to update Partner finder pages. Since BusinessReach is an online community, each time one of your Resellers adds a new location, this information will be automatically updated on your partner pages, eliminating errors and increasing customer traction.

      • Leverage the lead and product management systems within BusinessReach to greatly simplify how you share information about your products and send product updates

      • If your existing Resellers and Distributors are in our system, connecting with them in BusinessReach enables these Suppliers to provide information about their products and services to you in a way that greatly simplifies how you manage your business.

      • Utilize our marketing campaign capabilities to increase awareness of your products to Individuals, Buyers, Resellers and Distributors
