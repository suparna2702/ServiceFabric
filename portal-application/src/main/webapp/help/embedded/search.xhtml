Embedding BusinessReach Partner Search into a web site

BusinessReach Partner Search enable BusinessReach members who have signed up for Business profiles to embed two powerful features into their own web sites. These features enable businesses to setup partner search pages simply by adding several lines of HTML into their web site. All information on the web site is then dynamically updated from the partner network set up on Business Reach.

Follow the simple steps below to set up Partner Search:

  1. Find your search wizards URLs under Account &rrar; Embedded Portal Settings. Note that these URLs are unique to each Business Profile set up on BusinessReach.
  2. Select the Embedded Search URL and copy it onto your clipboard.
  3. Validate your URL by pasteing it into your browser URL box. You should see a form similar to below:

  4. If you do not see this page, go back to copy the URL again or contact BusinessReach support at support@businessreach-inc.com and provide your business email address as registered in BusinessReach.
  5. Copy the HTML below, and replace the text YOUR URL HERE with your copied URL. You may modify the width and height fields to match the page on your site.

    <iframe width="950" height="700" src="YOUR URL HERE" frameborder="0" scrolling="auto"></iframe>

  6. Embed the updated HTML from above into your web site, to an area where there is a large open space.
  7. Update your web site with the new HTML.
  8. Test the embedded page. You should see the image above again.
