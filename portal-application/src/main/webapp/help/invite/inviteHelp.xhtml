Invite Help

Invite can be used to invite your existing Business Partners and employees to be part of the Business Reach network to share documents and information, and view their updates

Select Connect from the Main Menu

Select Invite from the Drop down menu

Invite page appears, enter the Information for the Employee you wish to invite.

Or you can click on Partner Invite icon to go to the page to invite a Business Partner. Current restriction is that a Business Partner business partner would have to be external to Business Reach network.

Invited employees receive an email invitation and upon accepting, you are notified. The invited persons info appears in your “Connections” list. .
