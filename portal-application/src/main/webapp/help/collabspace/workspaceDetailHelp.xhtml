Workspace Details Help

Collaboration Workspace is a private network where employees and partners can be invited for collaboration on partner business processes. Currently you can share documents among invited participants of the Collaboration Workspace. When participants downloads, views or comments on shared documents all the participants of the workspace receives notification of the activity which creates visibility among participants on activities

Select Collaboration Workspace from Collaborate Menu item

Then click (+) tool bar button to create a Collaboration Workspace

Once a Collaboration Workspace is created click View button to open the workspace.

You can use the tool bar to invite, share document into the Workspace.
